Grilled Chicken & Avocado Salad

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 10 mins | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 Grilled Chicken Breast, sliced
  • 1 ripe Avocado, diced
  • 4 cups Mixed Salad Greens
  • 1/2 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons Olive oil & Lemon juice dressing

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the salad greens thoroughly, then place them in a large serving bowl.
  2. Add the halved cherry tomatoes and diced avocado to the greens.
  3. Top the salad with warm, freshly sliced grilled chicken breast.
  4. Drizzle the olive oil and fresh lemon juice dressing evenly over the salad.
  5. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.
