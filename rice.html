Tasty Rice and Beans with Tomato Stew

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 40 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups White Rice
  • 1 cup Honey Beans
  • Fried Tomato & Pepper Stew base
  • Your choice of cooked Beef, Chicken, or Fish
  • Salt and Seasoning to taste

Instructions

  1. Pick through the beans and boil them first for about 20 minutes until they start softening.
  2. Wash your white rice thoroughly and add it into the same pot with the boiling beans. Add hot water if needed, season with a pinch of salt, and cover.
  3. Cook on medium heat until both the rice and beans are soft and the water has completely dried up.
  4. Warm up your prepared tomato and pepper stew base with your cooked protein (beef/chicken/fish).
  5. Serve the fluffy rice and beans combo steaming hot with a generous scoop of the savory stew right over it!
