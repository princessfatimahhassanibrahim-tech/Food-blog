Classic Beef Tacos

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 15 mins | Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 packet Taco Seasoning
  • 6-8 Taco Shells (hard or soft)
  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • Salsa and Sour cream for topping

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm your taco shells in the oven or microwave for a minute until toasted.
  4. Spoon the seasoned beef into each taco shell.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, cheddar cheese, salsa, and a dollop of sour cream.
