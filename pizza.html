Classic Pepperoni Pizza

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 12 mins | Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made Pizza Dough ball
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 1.5 cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Pepperoni slices
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Brush a baking sheet or pizza stone with olive oil.
  2. Roll and stretch the pizza dough out into a large circle on your prepared surface.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce, then layer the pepperoni slices on top.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
