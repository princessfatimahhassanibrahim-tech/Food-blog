Authentic African Masa (Rice Cakes)

Prep: 8 hours (soaking) | Cook: 20 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Raw Tuwo Rice (short grain rice)
  • 1 tablespoon Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 cup Cooked Rice
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • A pinch of Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash the raw rice and soak it in water overnight or for at least 6-8 hours. Drain completely.
  2. Blend the soaked raw rice, cooked rice, chopped onions, and a little water together until you get a smooth, thick batter.
  3. Stir in the yeast and sugar. Cover the bowl and let it rise in a warm place for 2-3 hours until it becomes puffy and bubbly.
  4. Add a pinch of salt to the batter and stir gently.
  5. Grease your Masa pan lines with a drop of oil. Pour the batter into the cups, flip when bubbles form on top, and fry until both sides are golden brown!
