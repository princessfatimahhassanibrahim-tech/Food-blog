Spicy Goat Meat Pepper Soup

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 40 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Goat Meat (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons Pepper Soup Spice mix
  • 1 tablespoon Scent leaves or Utazi leaves (finely chopped)
  • 2 Scotch Bonnet peppers (Rodo), blended
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped
  • 2 Seasoning cubes & Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash the goat meat thoroughly. Place it in a pot with the chopped onions, seasoning cubes, and a little salt. Cover and steam for 10 minutes in its own juices.
  2. Add enough water to submerge the meat fully, then cook on medium heat until the meat starts getting tender.
  3. Add the pepper soup spice mix and the blended scotch bonnet pepper. Add more water if you prefer more broth.
  4. Simmer for another 15 minutes until the meat is perfectly soft and the broth is rich and spicy.
  5. Stir in the chopped scent leaves, cook for 2 more minutes, and serve hot!
