Classic Beef Burger

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 8 mins | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Ground Beef (80/20 mix)
  • 2 Burger Buns
  • 2 slices Cheddar Cheese
  • Lettuce leaves and Tomato slices
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Divide the ground beef into two equal portions and shape them into patties slightly wider than your buns.
  2. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on the first side.
  4. Flip the patties, immediately place a slice of cheese on each, and cook for another 3 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Toast the buns lightly, then assemble your burger with lettuce, tomato, the patty, and your favorite sauces.
