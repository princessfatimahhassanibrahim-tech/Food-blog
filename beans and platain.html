Homemade Plantain and Beans (Ewa Alata & Dodo)

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 45 mins | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Honey Beans (Oloyin)
  • 2 ripe Plantains
  • 1 large Onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Ground Crayfish
  • 1/2 cup Palm Oil
  • Blended Pepper mix (Atatodo and Rodo)
  • Salt and Seasoning cubes to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the beans thoroughly and cook in a pot with chopped onions until completely tender. Add water as needed.
  2. While beans are cooking, peel the plantains, slice them into cubes or rounds, and fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Once the beans are soft, add the palm oil, blended pepper mix, crayfish, and seasoning cubes. Stir well.
  4. Cover and let it simmer together on medium-low heat for 10-15 minutes so the flavors combine. Season with salt to taste.
  5. Serve the hot porridge beans with the fried dodo on top or on the side!
