Hot Egusi Soup with Semo

Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 35 mins | Servings: 5

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Melon seeds (Egusi), ground
  • Pre-cooked Beef, Shaki, or Dry Fish
  • 1 cup Palm Oil
  • Blended Pepper mix (Tatase, Rodo, and Onions)
  • 2 tablespoons Ground Crayfish
  • 2 cups Chopped Ugu leaves or Bitterleaf
  • Seasoning cubes and Salt

Instructions

  1. Mix your ground egusi powder with a little water in a bowl to form a thick, smooth paste.
  2. Heat palm oil in a pot on medium heat. Drop the egusi paste into the oil in small lumps and fry gently for about 5-8 minutes, stirring frequently so it doesn't burn.
  3. Pour in the blended pepper mix and the stock/broth from your cooked meat. Stir well and let it simmer for 15 minutes until the oil starts rising to the top.
  4. Add your cooked meat, fish, crayfish, and seasoning cubes. Reduce the heat and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in your chopped Ugu or bitterleaf, simmer for 3 minutes, and take it off the heat. Serve with freshly prepared Semo!
