Fudge Chocolate Cake

Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 30 mins | Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup Milk & 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 large Egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Add the milk, oil, and egg to the dry ingredients. Whisk together until the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let it cool before slicing.
